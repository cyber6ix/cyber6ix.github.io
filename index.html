Endpoint Protection Platform (EPP)

Implements preventive security controls like antivirus, firewalls, device control, and application allow/blocklists.

Antivirus and Antimalware

Basic protection against known viruses, worms, trojans, and other forms of malware.

Firewall

Controls incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules.

Data Encryption

Ensures that data on the device is encrypted and protected from unauthorized access.

Device Control

Manages and secures access to external devices such as USB drives.

Application Control

Restricts the execution of unauthorized applications to reduce the risk of malicious software.

Vulnerability Management

Identifies and mitigates potential vulnerabilities within the endpoint's operating system and applications.

Patch Management

Automates the process of applying patches to ensure that software is up to date and secure.
